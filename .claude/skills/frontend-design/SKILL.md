# Frontend Design Skill for Gifit

## Brand Direction

Gifit is an emotional gifting service that helps the sender express their feelings through a curated package of three fixed items:

1. Preserved flower
2. Candle / scent
3. Message card

Gifit is not a general gift shop.
Gifit is not a personality test.
Gifit does not diagnose the receiver.

Gifit helps the sender make their thoughtfulness visible.

The receiver should feel:

"이 사람이 나를 떠올리며 이런 고민을 했구나."

## Core Concept

The core of Gifit is not product recommendation.
The core is emotional translation.

The sender answers three questions:

* 이 사람의 분위기는?
* 지금 전하고 싶은 마음은?
* 이 선물을 보내는 계기가 있나요?

Then Gifit translates those answers into:

* a scent choice
* a preserved flower choice
* a message card that explains why those choices were made

The receiver does not simply receive a candle and a flower.
The receiver receives the trace of the sender’s thought process.

Core message:

"선물은 물건보다, 나를 생각했다는 흔적에서 오래 남습니다."

Alternative message:

"캔들과 꽃이 아니라, 당신을 떠올린 이유를 보냅니다."

## What Gifit Is

Gifit is:

* a service for expressing the sender’s heart
* a gift package built around choice reasons
* a way to show thoughtfulness
* a package of scent, preserved flower, and message card
* a gift experience where the message card is the emotional core

Gifit is not:

* an animal personality type test
* a psychological diagnosis service
* a generic gift recommendation mall
* a broad package commerce service with many random items
* a service that analyzes the receiver’s emotional state

## Fixed Gift Items

### 1. Preserved Flower

A preserved flower is a real flower that does not wither easily.
It can be shipped by regular parcel delivery.

Why it matters:

* Flowers have symbolic meanings.
* Flower language makes it easy to explain why this flower was chosen.
* Unlike fresh flowers, preserved flowers can last 1 to 3 years.
* It becomes a lasting emotional object.

### 2. Candle / Scent

Scent is the strongest medium for memory and emotion.

Why it matters:

* Scent can express atmosphere.
* The phrase "당신의 분위기를 닮은 향" makes emotional sense.
* The sender choosing a scent while thinking about the receiver becomes the emotional point.
* Scent makes the gift feel personal and intimate.

### 3. Message Card

The message card is the core of Gifit.

Why it matters:

* Without the card, the package is just a candle and a flower.
* The card does not explain the product.
* The card explains the sender’s feeling and the reason behind the selected scent and flower.
* The card should make the receiver feel: "이 사람이 나를 이렇게 생각했구나."

## Service Flow

### Step 1 — Emotion Input

The sender answers 3 questions.

Question 1:
이 사람의 분위기는?

Options:

* 밝고 활기찬
* 조용하고 차분한
* 따뜻하고 포근한
* 세련되고 도시적인

Question 2:
지금 전하고 싶은 마음은?

Options:

* 위로
* 응원
* 감사
* 축하
* 그리움
* 사과

Question 3:
이 선물을 보내는 계기가 있나요?

This can be a choice or short text input.

### Step 2 — Curation

Based on the sender’s answers, Gifit creates choice reasons.

Candle / scent:
"당신이 전하고 싶은 감정이 OO이라서, 이 향을 골랐어요."

Preserved flower:
"이 꽃의 꽃말이 OO이라서, 이 꽃을 골랐어요."

Message card:
Gifit connects the reason for the scent and the meaning of the flower into one emotional message.

### Step 3 — Sender Approval

The sender reviews the curation result and approves the order.

### Step 4 — Receiver Experience

The receiver receives:

* preserved flower
* candle / scent
* message card

The card contains the sender’s feeling and the reason behind the choices.

## Visual Mood

The design should feel:

* premium
* warm
* emotional
* elegant
* modern
* Korean startup landing page style
* gift-brand-like
* romantic but not overly sentimental
* trustworthy enough for commerce

Avoid:

* generic AI SaaS look
* random gradients
* childish pastel overload
* overly complex layouts
* stock-photo-heavy design
* too many decorative elements
* personality-test-like visuals
* animal type visuals
* diagnostic or psychological analysis visuals

## Color System

Use:

* warm ivory
* deep charcoal
* muted rose
* soft beige
* warm gray
* dusty pink accents

Recommended color tokens:

* background: #FAF7F2
* foreground: #1F1B18
* muted: #7A7069
* card: #FFFFFF
* accent: #C58A7A
* accent-soft: #F0D8D1
* border: #E8DDD6

## Typography

Use a clear Korean-friendly type hierarchy.

Suggested font direction:

* Pretendard or system sans-serif
* Large emotional headline
* Clean body text
* High readability

Typography scale:

* Hero headline: 52px desktop / 36px mobile
* Section headline: 36px desktop / 28px mobile
* Card title: 22px
* Body: 16px to 18px
* Caption: 14px

Use strong line-height:

* Headlines: 1.15
* Body: 1.6 to 1.75

## Spacing

Use an 8px spacing system.

Typical section padding:

* Desktop: 96px to 140px vertical
* Mobile: 64px to 88px vertical

Keep layouts spacious.
Do not overcrowd sections.

## Component Style

Cards:

* Rounded corners: 24px to 32px
* Soft shadow
* Thin warm border
* Ivory or white background
* Subtle hover lift

Buttons:

* Primary button: warm rose or deep charcoal
* Rounded full or large radius
* Smooth hover transition
* Clear CTA

Forms:

* Large readable input fields
* Soft border
* Calm and premium feeling

## Motion

Use Framer Motion.
Animations should be subtle and premium.

Use:

* fade up on scroll
* staggered card reveal
* soft hover lift
* slight scale on CTA hover
* gentle floating motion only when appropriate

Avoid:

* bouncy animations
* overly flashy motion
* too many moving elements

## Landing Page Structure

Recommended sections:

1. Sticky navbar
2. Hero section
3. Problem section
4. Solution section
5. Service flow section
6. Curation result example section
7. Fixed package items section
8. Receiver experience section
9. Differentiation from Kakao Gift

11. FAQ
12. Final CTA
13. Footer

## Copy Direction

Use Korean copy.
Make it emotional but clear.

Avoid vague phrases like:

* 감성적인 선물
* 특별한 경험
* 마음을 담은 선물

Prefer concrete phrases like:

* 왜 이 향과 꽃을 골랐는지까지 함께 전합니다.
* 보내는 사람의 마음을 향, 꽃말, 메시지 카드로 번역합니다.
* 카카오 선물하기가 상품을 보낸다면, Gifit은 선택의 이유를 함께 보냅니다.
* 받는 사람은 캔들과 꽃이 아니라, 자신을 떠올린 고민의 흔적을 받습니다.
